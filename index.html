Welcome to Memory Game!

Introducing a thrilling memory card game with pictures! Test your concentration, observation, and memory skills as you match pairs of captivating images hidden beneath a grid. Flip cards, uncover matches, and aim to reveal all pairs in the fewest turns. Enjoy hours of fun and mental exercise with this classic game for all ages.

  1. Start the game with all cards facedown.
  2. Flip two cards at a time by clicking on them.
  3. If the cards match, they stay face-up and you earn points.
  4. If the cards don't match, they flip back facedown.
  5. Keep flipping cards until all pairs are matched.
  6. Be more successful by completing the game in fewer attemps and in a shortest time.
  7. Restart the game to shuffle the cards and reset the score.
